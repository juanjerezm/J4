# Financial Data

## Exchange Rates

The DKK/EUR exchange rate is taken from [Danmarks Nationalbank](https://nationalbanken.statbank.dk/statbank5a/selectvarval/define.asp?PLanguage=1&MainTable=DNVALA). The average exchange rate in 2025 corresponds to 7.4634 DKK/EUR (equivalently, 0.1340 EUR/DKK).

## Discount Rate

The discount rate is assumed to be equal to the average interest rate paid by Danish non-financial corporations on new domestic bank loans in DKK with maturities over 1 year and loan sizes above 7.5 million DKK. This reflects the financing conditions relevant for large, long-term infrastructure investments such as district heating networks and waste-heat recovery systems.

Based on monthly data from Danmarks Nationalbank for 2025, the average annualised agreed rate across the year was approximately 3.6%. A discount rate of **4%** is therefore adopted.

Source: Danmarks Nationalbank, [*Nye indenlandske udlån ekskl. kassekreditter o.l. fra pengeinstitutter* (DNRNUPI)](https://www.statistikbanken.dk/DNRNUPI).
