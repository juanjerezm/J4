# Other Data

## EU ETS quota price

Data is obtained from the European Energy Exchange (EEX), which operates the primary auction platform for EU ETS allowances. More information is available on the [EEX EU ETS auctions page](https://www.eex.com/en/markets/environmental-markets/eu-ets-auctions).

The specific dataset is downloaded from the [EEX EU ETS auction report (2025)](https://public.eex-group.com/eex/eua-auction-report/emission-spot-primary-market-auction-report-2025-data.xlsx) and stored as `data/_master-data/eu-ets-quota-price.xlsx`.

The quota price used is the 2025 volume-weighted average: **73.43 €/tonne-CO₂**.
